About Me

I'm a PhD-level epidemiologist with seven years of combined laboratory and applied research experience, specializing in global disease epidemiology, vaccine science, and policy. My work bridges rigorous statistical analysis—including longitudinal and survival methods—with real-world program evaluation.

Technical skills include R, SAS, and STATA for data analysis, alongside program planning, monitoring, and evaluation expertise. I’m fluent in French and Arabic and have practical knowledge of Swahili.

About the Course

Generative AI for Work & Research is a 2‑month guided program delivered by GRAPH Courses. It combines weekly live workshops, hands‑on help sessions, private community forums, personalized feedback, and capstone coaching. Graduates earn a shareable certificate and lifetime access to tutorial materials.

Bootcamp Features

  • No‑code AI tools for task automation
  • Live workshops & help sessions
  • Personalized assessment feedback
  • Capstone project mentoring
  • Shareable certificate upon completion

Course Modules

Module 1 – Websites, Documents & Presentations

  • How LLMs are trained & major providers
  • Generating Markdown, LaTeX, HTML
  • No‑code research websites
  • AI tools for scientific communication

Module 2 – AI‑Automated Data Analysis

  • Quick data analysis with ChatGPT Code Interpreter
  • Automated dashboards with TheBricks
  • Dataset processing with Julius
  • Automated reports & visualizations

Module 3 – GenAI for Research & Text Analysis

  • Efficient literature reviews
  • Text classification & sentiment analysis
  • Knowledge maps & summarization workflows

Module 4 – Custom GPTs & Chatbots

  • LLM architecture, context windows, RAG
  • Building domain‑specific GPTs
  • Interactive chatbots with Botpress

Module 5 – AI for Task Automation

  • Email response automations
  • AI agents for literature monitoring
  • Browser control workflows

Module 6 – GenAI for Communication & Outreach

  • AI‑generated research summaries
  • Scientific figures & diagrams
  • Video abstracts with minimal effort

Module 7 – Capstone Project & Showcase

  • Peer feedback & coaching
  • Final project presentation
  • Public Q&A session

Assessment & Grading

  • Online quizzes – 15%
  • Workshop assignments – 35%
  • Attendance & participation – 10%
  • Final project – 40%
  • Passing grade: 80% (attend ≥5 workshops)
